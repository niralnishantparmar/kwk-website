About Our Visualization

All three parts are embed and interactive, and made in Tableau

Click on the filters to navigate through the visualization!

This visualization is interactive so the user can engage with the information. The user can focus on data from different countries and years. The three graphs emphasize the difference men and women faces in education and overall their jobs.

From our data, we can conclude that women’s access to education, or lack thereof, is a large problem globally; this hinders them from entering STEM fields, and prevents them from having a say in the conversation. Overall, we hope our project was insightful! We encourage you to continue fighting to close the gender gap in STEM fields, and advocate for women all around the world.
