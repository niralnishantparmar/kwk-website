Women’s Education and the Gender Gap in STEM Careers

Empowering futures through equal opportunity in science and technology.

Women vs Men in STEM Fields

In a world where technology is increasingly important, it’s not fair for STEM to be a male-dominated field. Women all around the globe are missing out on opportunities due to the barriers they face, namely seen through a lack of access to education. Our project hopes to shed light on this issue, displaying just how large the gender disparity is.
